FAQ about The Witcher

How long does a Witcher live?

Witchers can live to be over 150 years old, but probably a lot longer. Witchers are often viewed with fear and suspicion by the common folk.

Is Ciri a witcher?

Ciri does undergo intense training to become a Witcher herself.

How old is Geralt?

A five-year-old Geralt was one of those young initiates, having just endured the Trial of the Grasses. By the time Geralt appears in the first episode of the Netflix series The Witcher, he is already 71, though he is played by 36-year-old Henry Cavill.

Is Ciri Geralts daughter?

Despite their white hair and resemblance, Ciri and Geralt are not related to each other by blood. Their destinies are however intertwined. Ciri is Geralt's adoptive daughter because of the Law of Surprise.

Why is Geralt's hair white?

Geralt exhibited great tolerance to mutagens that grant Witchers their abilities, especially in the Trial of the Grasses. As a result, he was put through further experiments that caused him to lose all pigmentation in his body, turning his skin and hair pale white.
